Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

This app (Sessco / Barth) uses the Meta Marketing API to create and manage ads on behalf of authorized business accounts. We do not collect or store personal data from end users of Meta platforms.

2. Data we use

We use only what is necessary to run ad campaigns:

  • Meta ad account IDs and access tokens (stored securely by the business operator)
  • Campaign, ad set, and ad metadata (names, budgets, targeting) sent to Meta via the API
  • Video and image creatives uploaded to Meta for ads

We do not sell or share this data with third parties except as required to operate the Meta Marketing API.

3. Data storage

Configuration (e.g. account IDs and tokens) is stored in the business’s own environment. We do not maintain a separate database of Meta user data.

4. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the business that operates this app (e.g. the contact email in your Meta app settings).
